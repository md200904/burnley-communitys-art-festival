Burnley Community's Art Festival

The artist & maker community of Burnley

Burnley's Community Arts Festival is a group of events that look to showcase the vast life of arts and crafts in Burnley, showcasing local artists and more.

All events are listed in the events section for you to look at, along with images from some of our events and helpful indicators on the time, date and place of said events.
